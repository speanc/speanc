Morning Routine Personal Project

Objective

Create a visually clean series of designs depicting 3 phrases all ending in the word “UP”

Reflection

After falling in love with Blender, I found myself tackling designs with it that I would have otherwise worked on in Illustrator, which has given me a whole new world of designs. The message behind it has a negative vibe, which is juxtaposed by the pristine whiteness of the design.
